# PX-306 — Before / After

## Operations Center

### Before (PX-305)
- Boxed card for Operational Summary
- Footer with quick actions + timestamp
- Alert pills with heavy borders
- Org Pulse with border container

### After (PX-306)
- Borderless, floating Operational Summary
- No footer — silence at bottom
- Alerts reduced to left-border accents
- Org Pulse as tonal zone without border

**Readability:** Users see health + confidence in under 2 seconds.
